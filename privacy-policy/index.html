    Privacy Policy

    Effective Date: May 23, 2026

    Infodoor (“we”, “us”, “our”) is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal and corporate data. This Privacy Policy details our data governance practices when you visit our website infodoor.com.sg or interact with our mobile applications and AI advisory services.

    🔒 1. Data Minimization and Protection

    We adhere strictly to international data privacy principles (including Singapore’s Personal Data Protection Act - PDPA, GDPR, and California’s CCPA).

    • We do not sell, rent, or lease your personal or corporate data to third parties.
    • All data is stored in encrypted cloud environments with rigorous role-based access control (RBAC).

    🔎 2. Information We Collect

    A. General Technical Data

    When browsing our website, we collect standard log data (including your IP address, browser type, and page access timestamps) to analyze site performance and maintain system security.

    B. Inquiry and Communication Details

    If you contact us via email (info@infodoor.com.sg), we collect your name, corporate email address, and any project files or technical scopes you provide. This information is strictly used to address your inquiries and formulate estimates.

    🤖 3. Data Governance for AI Services

    When implementing custom Artificial Intelligence pipelines or Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) models for your organization:

    • All model queries and training data remain locked inside your secure enterprise cloud networks.
    • We do not use customer data to train public foundation models.

    🛠️ 4. Cookies and Web Analytics

    We utilize basic cookies and lightweight web analytics tools to verify sitemap crawls and improve page loading times. You can disable cookies in your browser settings without restricting access to our information.

    🤝 5. Contact Our Privacy Officer

    If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy or wish to request data erasure, please contact our Data Governance Officer at: info@infodoor.com.sg.
